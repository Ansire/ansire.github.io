Short Bio

I am a PhD candidate in School of Creative Media, City University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Hongbo Fu. Previously, I obtained my bachelor degree in Information Management and Systems at Dalian University of Technology. My research interests fall in the fields of interactive computer graphics, image synthesis and deep neural works.
